3. Watcher behavior and failure modes - PASS. The watcher remains opt-in, uses chokidar only for datasets that enable it, applies source filters plus secret/build/cache exclusions consistently, performs startup reconcile before arming, debounces path changes into final-state batches, fails loudly on inotify limits without silent polling fallback, and drops event-flood batches with structured warning rather than embedding runaway churn. Evidence: [saivage/SPEC/2026-05/rag-subsystem-design/F01-rag-subsystem/04-addendum-r2.md#L204-L214](saivage/SPEC/2026-05/rag-subsystem-design/F01-rag-subsystem/04-addendum-r2.md#L204-L214).
