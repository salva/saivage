No blocking finding. r4 covers provider outage behavior, retryable quota failures, model drift, poisoned content, secret indexing, sidecar tradeoffs, provider key handling, disk corruption, PII, backup/restore, and concrete failure walkthroughs ([saivage/SPEC/2026-05/rag-subsystem-design/F01-rag-subsystem/01-analysis-r4.md](saivage/SPEC/2026-05/rag-subsystem-design/F01-rag-subsystem/01-analysis-r4.md#L638-L784)).
